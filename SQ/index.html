How the QB works
1
Real oral questions, sourced from the preparation group
Every question is reported by a candidate who sat in front of an actual Kochi MMD examiner. No invented scenarios — if it's in the QB, someone was asked it in an oral exam.
2
Validated against primary IMO instruments before publishing
Every answer is cross-checked against SOLAS, MARPOL, ISM Code, IS Code, IBC Code, IACS URs, and DGS circulars before it goes live. Where a common answer is wrong, a correction note is embedded — not silently replaced.
3
Study the card anatomy, not just the answer
Open the 15-second answer first — that's your oral exam opener. Then the 60-second version. Then explore the deep-dive blocks at your own pace. The Trap Questions block is where most repeats are prevented.
4
Spot an error? Report it and get credited
Every card has a direct correction link. Submit with your rank and vessel type. All corrections are reviewed against the primary instrument. If accepted, your name goes into the version history.
Kochi MMD examiner profiles
CE Nair
Conventions · MARPOL · PSC
Asks for regulation numbers verbatim — "MARPOL Annex I, Regulation 14", not just "MARPOL". Strict on PSC deficiency categories. Will ask what specific document is deficient, not just what rule applies.
CE Simon
Machinery · 2-Stroke
Asks for diagram + operating principle. Hands you a marker and says "draw the scavenging process". Focuses on failure analysis and root cause — not just what broke, but why.
CE Rajappan
Stability · Cargo
Draws the GZ curve on the board and asks you to label all key points. Expects exact IS Code criteria — 0.055, 0.09, 0.03. Links stability directly to container stack weight and port conditions.
CE Srivastava
Electrotechnology
Circuit diagram questions — earth fault detection, protection relay coordination, UMS alarm logic. Asks for the philosophy behind the design, not just the name of the component.
CE Senthil
Safety · LSA · MOB
Procedure sequence questions — "walk me through MOB from first sighting." Expects the SOLAS regulation number for every LSA requirement. Will stop you mid-answer to ask the reg citation.
