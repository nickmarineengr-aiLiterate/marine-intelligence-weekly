🛠️ Why this Question Bank Exists

This isn't just another business—it's a community resource project I built because I couldn't find one when I needed it.

Like many MEO Class I candidates, I was tired of searching through scattered notes, old PDFs, WhatsApp forwards, and expensive coaching material. Many training resources cost ₹40,000–₹50,000 and continue to become more expensive. I kept asking myself:

Why can't experienced marine engineers build something better together? Why can't we document the practical knowledge we've gained at sea and create a living resource that helps every Indian marine engineer preparing for MEO Class I?

I'm a serving Second Engineer with Maersk and am preparing for my own MEO Class I examinations. Everything on this platform is built from that perspective—creating the resource I wish had existed when I started my preparation.

Your ₹899 isn't just paying for access to a question bank. It helps keep the platform online, continuously updated, secure, and free from advertisements, while supporting the addition of new questions and answers as candidates share their oral experiences.

As the community grows, the platform will continue to improve, and pricing may change in the future to support additional features, stronger security, and ongoing development.

This project exists today because a small group believed in the idea before it became a website—school friends, college classmates, juniors, and fellow MEO Class II candidates who simply said, "Build it. We'll support you."

I'm building this platform largely on my own, alongside my sailing career. That means improvements won't appear overnight, but they will continue to come. Every suggestion from members is read, considered, and, where practical, gradually implemented. My goal isn't to release everything at once—it's to build a reliable, continuously improving resource for the marine engineering community.

If you've joined today, you're now part of that journey too.

Thank you for your trust and support.

Fair winds, and all the very best for your MEO Class I journey.
