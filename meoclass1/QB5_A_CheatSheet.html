15-Second Quick-Fire Index — All 20 Questions
Q1–Q20 — One-Line Oral Anchors Memorise before oral
Q1 — Human Element & HTW
75–96% of casualties involve human factors. HTW Sub-committee (est. 2013) develops STCW standards, model courses, and embeds human factors in all IMO instruments. Mandated by A.947(23), ISM §6, STCW Manila 2010.
Q2 — ERRM
Engine Room Resource Management — effective use of all resources (human, equipment, information, external) for safe operations. STCW 2010 A-III/1 and A-III/2. IMO Model Course 2.07.
Q3 — Johari Window
4-quadrant self-awareness model: Arena (known to both), Blind Spot (others know, self doesn't), Façade (self knows, others don't), Unknown (nobody knows). CE goal: expand Arena.
Q4 — Maslow's Hierarchy
5 levels: Physiological → Safety → Social → Esteem → Self-Actualisation. Lower needs must be met first. MLC (rest/food/medical) covers Levels 1–2. Start diagnosis from Level 1 always.
Q5 — Motivation Theories
Maslow 1943 / Herzberg 1959 (hygiene + motivators) / McGregor 1960 (X=control, Y=trust) / Vroom 1964. Vroom formula: Motivation = Expectancy × Instrumentality × Valence.
Q6 — Conflict Management
Thomas-Kilmann 5 styles: Competing / Collaborating (CE default) / Compromising / Avoiding / Accommodating. Safety = Competing. Team decisions = Collaborating. Never Avoiding for safety issues.
Q7 — TQM
Total Quality Management — continuous improvement at every level. PDCA cycle (Plan-Do-Check-Act = Deming Wheel). QA = proactive/process (prevent). QC = reactive/product (detect). ISM Code is maritime TQM.
Q8 — Decision-Making Tools
FORDEC (Facts-Options-Risks-Decision-Execution-Check) for complex decisions. OODA Loop for emergencies. Risk Matrix (Risk = L×C) for quantification. Biases: anchoring, confirmation bias, authority gradient.
Q9 — Organisational Behaviour
Tuckman's Stages: Forming→Storming→Norming→Performing→Adjourning. Large crew change under port pressure can jump from Forming to aggressive Storming — CE must mediate early and decisively.
Q10 — Risk vs Hazard
Hazard = source of potential harm (static). Risk = likelihood × consequence (dynamic). Risk changes with controls applied. Risk = L × C is the basis for every PTW and RA onboard.
Q11 — Risk Assessment
9-step process: identify hazards → evaluate L×C → risk category (1–6 OK, 7–12 ALARP, 13+ unacceptable) → hierarchy of controls → PTW → toolbox talk → execute → close. H₂S limit: 1 ppm TLV-TWA.
Q12 — Root Cause Analysis
5-Whys (simple), Fishbone/Ishikawa-6Ms (complex), Fault Tree Analysis (systems), Bowtie (barriers). Always reach a system failure root cause — never stop at "crew negligence". SMART corrective actions.
Q13 — Appraisal of JE
6 steps: pre-appraisal prep → private meeting → SMART targets → document and sign → submit to company → monitor. JE has right to add comments (MLC 5.1.5). Connects to Johari, Vroom, Maslow.
Q14 — Bloom's Taxonomy
2001 revision (Anderson & Krathwohl): Remember→Understand→Apply→Analyse→Evaluate→Create. Note: Create is apex (Synthesis/Evaluation swapped). MEO oral tests at Analyse/Evaluate level.
Q15 — Mentoring
Long-term developmental relationship (career, identity, tacit knowledge). vs Coaching (performance, shorter-term) vs Training (skill, structured). Reverse mentoring: junior teaches senior on digital tools, new regs.
Q16 — TEAP
Training, Examination and Assessment Programme — DGS India's national instrument fulfilling STCW obligation. Part A (2015) = framework/certification; Part B (2019) = course curricula. MS (STCW) Rules 2014 is the statutory basis.
Q17 — META Manual
Maritime Education and Training in India. Vol. I = policy/MTI governance. Vol. II = delivery standards/instructor quality. TEAP says WHAT to teach; META says HOW to teach it.
Q18 — VICT
Vertical Integration Course for Trainers. Vertical = full Bloom spectrum coverage. Integration = instructional design + assessment fused in one qualification. DGS Order TR/CR/26 of 2018. Replaces TOTI. IMO MC 6.09.
Q19 — TOTA & TOTI
TOTI (instruction) + TOTA (assessment) — introduced DGS Order 5 of 2013. Replaced by VICT+AECS under TR/CR/25 (AECS) + TR/CR/26 (VICT) of 2018. Problems: overlap, misalignment with IMO MCs, instruction-assessment split.
Q20 — CE Duties
STCW A-III/2 (competency) · SOLAS Ch.V Reg.14 safe manning · MARPOL Annex I ORB signing (criminal liability) · MLC 2.3 rest hours · ISM §6 resources · MS Act §77 CoC · §225 pollution liability.
