CBDR вҖ” Constant Bearing, Decreasing Range Q29

CBDR вҖ” Risk of Collision Indicator
DefinitionIf compass bearing to another vessel remains constant AND range is decreasing вҶ’ risk of collision exists
COLREGS Rule 7Every available means shall be used to determine risk of collision. If bearing does not appreciably change вҖ” risk deemed to exist (Rule 7(d)(i))
Rule 8 ActionAction must be taken in ample time; must be large, positive, and made in good time
CE relevanceIn UMS вҖ” CE called to engine room for maneuvering; must understand bridge CBDR principle to respond correctly to manoeuvring orders
вҡ  CBDR Trap: "What if bearing changes slightly?" Answer: A small change in bearing does not eliminate risk вҖ” the vessel may still be on a collision course due to CPA (Closest Point of Approach) calculation.

15-Second Quick-Fire Index вҖ” Q21 to Q36
Q21вҖ“Q36 вҖ” One-Line Oral Anchors
Q21 вҖ” Taking Over as CE
Review vessel history вҶ’ handover from outgoing CE вҶ’ independent machinery rounds вҶ’ safety systems check вҶ’ manning/cert verification вҶ’ fuel/lube status вҶ’ SMS familiarisation вҶ’ sign CE Takeover Certificate. Never sign without documenting known defects.
Q22 вҖ” Conflict Management
Thomas-Kilmann: Competing (safety emergency) / Collaborating (CE default) / Compromising / Avoiding (never for safety) / Accommodating. MLC 5.1.5: CE must not suppress complaints. Document serious conflicts in Official Log Book.
Q23 вҖ” HR Management
CE HR functions: recruitment input (appraisals вҶ’ MLC 1.4), rest hour management (MLC 2.3), appraisal/development, conflict resolution, training planning (Bloom's levels), disciplinary action (MS Act), complaints handling (MLC 5.1.5).
Q24 вҖ” CE as Leader
Yes вҖ” STCW A-III/2 names "use leadership and managerial skills" as mandatory. Key theories: Situational (Hersey-Blanchard вҖ” match style to follower), Transformational (build safety culture), Transactional (routine compliance), Autocratic (emergencies only).
Q25 вҖ” CE & ISPS Audit
CE responsibilities: engine dept access control (restricted areas: ER, steering gear, COвӮӮ room), engine room security procedures in SSP, machinery readiness for emergency departure, security watch records, CCTV/alarm systems in engine scope.
Q26 вҖ” Fatigue
Fatigue = weariness from insufficient rest, disrupted circadian rhythm, excessive workload. Rest hours: 10 hrs/24 hrs; 77 hrs/7 days (MLC 2.3 + STCW VIII/1). Mitigation: scheduling, workload prioritisation, culture. MSC-MEPC.7/Circ.6.
Q27 вҖ” Fitness for Duty
Broader than fatigue вҖ” includes alcohol (BAC вүӨ0.05%; breath вүӨ0.25 mg/L per STCW VIII/2), drugs, medication, illness, injury, psychological distress. CE must relieve and not assign duty until fit. Document and inform Master.
Q28 вҖ” MACN
Maritime Anti-Corruption Network вҖ” founded 2011; 200+ members; >50% global tonnage. Combats facilitation payment demands via collective action, incident reporting, government engagement. UK Bribery Act: paying = criminal offence. Use MACN incident reporting system.
Q29 вҖ” CBDR
Constant Bearing, Decreasing Range = risk of collision exists (COLREGS Rule 7(d)(i)). Action per Rule 8: large, positive, early. CE relevance: understand manoeuvring orders in UMS вҖ” bridge CBDR triggers engine room actions.
Q30 вҖ” PID Controller
P = corrects current error; I = eliminates steady-state offset (integral of past error); D = predictive damping (rate of change). Formula: u(t) = Kp[e(t) + (1/Ti)вҲ«e(П„)dП„ + TdВ·de(t)/dt]. Locations: ME governor, boiler, HFO temp, refrigeration.
Q31 вҖ” 3-Element Controller
Boiler drum feedwater control. Three elements: (1) Drum Level (master вҖ” what we control), (2) Steam Flow (feedforward вҖ” anticipates demand), (3) Feed Flow (slave вҖ” inner loop precision). Switch to single-element below ~25% MCR.
Q32 вҖ” Ziegler-Nichols
Set I=вҲһ, D=0. Increase Kp to sustained oscillation вҶ’ record Ku (ultimate gain) and Pu (ultimate period). PID: Kp=0.6Ku, Ti=0.5Pu, Td=0.125Pu. De-tune by 20-30% before applying to live system.
Q33 вҖ” IG Alarms & OвӮӮ Analyser
Normal IG OвӮӮ: <5%. High OвӮӮ alarm: >5%. Trip: >8%. Other trips: scrubber low water, deck seal low water, high temp (>65В°C). OвӮӮ analyser: Span gas = calibrate scale; Zero gas (NвӮӮ) = set zero; Test gas = verify accuracy between calibrations.
Q34 вҖ” War Zone Crew Management
Apply Maslow Level 2 (safety) first вҖ” machinery readiness for evasive manoeuvring, damage control stations. Then Level 3 (social) вҖ” open communication, no information vacuum. Apply Vroom вҖ” clear timeline to safe passage. BMP5 procedures. ISPS Security Level elevation.
Q35 вҖ” Framo Pump RA & PTW
RA: identify hazards (hydraulic hose failure, hot work proximity, enclosed space atmosphere, electrical isolation). PTW scope: LOTO hydraulic + electrical, enclosed space entry if applicable (OвӮӮ 19.5-23.5%, HвӮӮS <1 ppm, CO <25 ppm). Continuous monitoring during operation. IMO A.1050(27).
Q36 вҖ” Human Element in Casualty
Human element factors in investigation: fatigue, situational awareness failure, communication breakdown, authority gradient, training gap, procedural violation vs error, workload overload, cultural factors, organisational pressures. Framework: HFACS. ISM В§9 + IMO MSC.255(84) Casualty Investigation Code.
