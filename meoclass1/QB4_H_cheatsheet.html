🧠 QB4_H Cheat Sheet — Retrieval Cues (Huberman/Sung Dual-Coding)

"AECS?" — STOP
↓
Assessment, Examination & Certification of SEAFARERS
↓
Trains EXAMINERS/surveyors/auditors/MTI faculty — NOT you as candidate
↓
Eligibility = CoC (no lower than examined rank) + TOTA cert
Security training ladderSAT (everyone) → DSD (assigned security role) → SSO (accountable to Master).
SSAS test — zero indication ruleNo buzzer, no light, ever. Confirmation only via CSO message to the Master.
Jamming vs SpoofingJamming = loud, obvious, alarms fire. Spoofing = silent, believable, no alarm — far more dangerous.
Statutory extension math5 yrs max validity → 3-month extension cap (1 month short-voyage) → Flag State only, never Class alone.
CoC vs CoPCoC = authorises a RANK. CoP = confirms a SKILL. Never call a CoP "just a course cert."
WHO document, correct nameSSCEC (clean) / SSCC (deficiency found) under IHR 2005. Never say "de-ratting certificate."
