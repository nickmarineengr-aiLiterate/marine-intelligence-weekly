It establishes a total ban on plastic discharges anywhere at sea and designates strict zero-discharge Special Areas.

MARPOLSURVEYIACS
15-Second Answer
The Hong Kong Convention (HKC) ensures that ships are recycled safely without endangering human health or the environment. Flag states must mandate, verify, and certify an Inventory of Hazardous Materials (IHM) for all vessels \ge 500 GT , tracking onboard hazardous materials from construction to demolition.

MARPOLISM
15-Second Answer
Environmentally Sound Management (ESM) means taking all practicable shipboard steps to minimize, manage, and dispose of hazardous waste safely to protect human health and the environment. It integrates compliance across the Basel Convention, Hong Kong Convention, and all MARPOL Annexes under a single operational standard.
