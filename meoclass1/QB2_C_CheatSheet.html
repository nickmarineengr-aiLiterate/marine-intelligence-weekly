For a container fire with unknown cargo, sound the general alarm, muster the crew, and isolate cargo hold ventilation. Deploy unmanned portable water monitors from an upwind position for heavy boundary cooling, and do not open the container doors or inject water blindly to avoid triggering a backdraft or violent chemical reaction.

A water mist lance is a specialized tool that penetrates a container&#x27;s wall to discharge a cooling mist inside.
